Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

Cooked, chilled rice

eggs

carrots, onions, green onions and peas

garlic

soy sauce

Oyster sauce

Toasted sesame oil

butter

Directions:

1. Scramble eggs

2. Saute veggies and garlic

3. Stir fry rice

4. Remove pan from heat

5. Taste and season

6. Serve!
